InstaView for WooCommerce

== Description ==

In a world where user experience is paramount, InstaView for WooCommerce is the perfect tool to elevate your WooCommerce store. This innovative plugin revolutionizes the way your customers interact with your products.

With InstaView, your customers can now effortlessly get an instant overview of your WooCommerce products. Its elegant and user-friendly design is what makes InstaView stand out. Whether it's a cascading product summary or a convenient popup window, your customers will have an immersive browsing experience. And don't worry about compatibility – InstaView for WooCommerce is fully responsive and boasts a mobile-friendly interface, ensuring that every customer has a seamless experience regardless of their device.

After all, it's not just about products; it's about giving your customers a glimpse of what's possible – a shopping journey that's engaging, customizable, and oh-so-smooth. Elevate your online store with InstaView and watch your customer engagement soar to new heights.

== InstaView for WooCommerce Features == 

Basic Features:

- Lightweight and User-Friendly: Our plugin is an efficient, easy-to-use, and sturdy solution for providing an - instant view of all WooCommerce products.

- InstaView Button Control: Effortlessly enable or disable the Insta View button and also for mobile view.

- Editable and Translatable Labels: Modify and translate button label text to match your preferences.

- InstaView Style Option: Choose between Insta View styles, such as a Button or Link, for your modal popup, providing flexibility in presentation.

- Mobile-Responsive Functionality: Enjoy seamless instant view functionality on mobile devices and iPads.

- Multilingual Support: Seamlessly integrates with WPML for building multilingual websites.

- Universal Compatibility: Works harmoniously with any WordPress theme and WooCommerce plugins.

- Cross-Browser Compatibility: Ensures smooth operation across all modern web browsers.

- Dedicated Support: Count on our dedicated support team for any assistance you may need.

- Regular Updates and Bug Fixes: Stay up to date with periodic updates and bug fixes for continued excellence.

== Installation ==

1. Install & activate the WooCommerce plugin, if not activated already. 
2. Upload the ZIP file through the 'Plugins > Add New > Upload' screen in your WordPress dashboard
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

In case you find difficulties in setting up your plugin, feel free to write an email to plugins@qewebby.com
